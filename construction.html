Construction & Challenges

From Culebra Cut to the locks and dams, disease control, landslides, and daily risk defined the build.

Culebra Cut

Excavation through the continental divide demanded round-the-clock work and constant responses to landslides.

Public Health

Sanitation campaigns targeted mosquito breeding, reducing yellow fever and malaria, and enabling sustained progress.

Locks and Dams

Engineers built paired locks to raise and lower ships using gravity-fed culverts and gates, an example of Progressive Era faith in expert planning.
