Timeline

Milestones from vision to completion and beyond.

  1. 1500s Early proposals to link the oceans across the isthmus.
  2. 1880–1889 French attempt led by Ferdinand de Lesseps fails.
  3. 1903 U.S. assumes control; lock-canal plan advances.
  4. 1904–1913 Massive excavation, dams, locks, and disease control.
  5. Aug 15, 1914 Official opening; SS Ancon transits.
  6. 1977–1999 Treaties lead to full Panamanian control.
