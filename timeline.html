Timeline

  1. 1500s Early visions for an isthmian route.
  2. 1880s French sea-level attempt fails (disease, landslides, finances).
  3. 1903 Panama’s independence; U.S. secures rights to build.
  4. 1904 U.S. construction begins; shift to lock design.
  5. 1913 Culebra Cut breakthrough; systems integrated.
  6. 1914 Official opening; SS Ancon transits.
  7. 1977–1999 Treaties and transfer of control to Panama.
  8. 2016 Expansion opens to New Panamax vessels.
